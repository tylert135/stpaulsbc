Pastoral Search Committee

Mission Statement
We will faithfully serve to hear and obey God's call for a Pastor for St. Paul's Baptist Church. We are striving to be obedient to God's will.


Applicants

On behalf of the Pastoral Search Committee of St. Paul's Baptist Church the search for a new pastor for our congregation is an act of spiritual discernment.  We covet your prayers in this process.

To assist us in our process of discernment, we have sought the guidance and support of our denominational leaders in the American Baptist Churches of Pennsylvania and Delaware.  All of the applicants who wish to be considered to become our new pastor must have an American Baptist Profile (resume).

If you do not have a profile created with the American Baptists, you will need to contact the American Baptist Churches USA denominational offices in Valley Forge, PA.   Please telephone Cindy Beard, American Baptist Personnel Services at 1-800-222-3872, Extension 2488.

You will also need to contact the Reverend Dr. Frank Frischkorn, Executive Regional Pastor for the American Baptist Churches of Pennsylvania and Delaware (ABCOPAD), through his secretary at 1-800-358-6999. Dr. Frischkorn will need to sponsor you into the ABPS system.

Thank you again for your interest and we look forward to considering all of those who have completed profiles.

Sincerely,

The Pastoral Search Committee
