Pastor Elect - Reverend Dr. Marjorie Duncan Reed


       Come See!

Reverend Dr. Marjorie Duncan Reed obtained a license to preach in July, 1985 and was ordained in April 1989 at the Antioch Baptist Church in North Hills. PA. She has an earned Bachelor’s Degree and Master’s Degree in Theology and an earned Doctorate Degree in Ministry awarded by Slidell Baptist Seminary.

She was voted in by the congregation to pastor at St. Paul's on Monday March 17, 2014.

St. Paul's Baptist Church, Conshohocken Pennsylvania
