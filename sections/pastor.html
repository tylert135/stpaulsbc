Reverend Doctor Marjorie Duncan Reed


Reverend Dr. Marjorie Duncan Reed obtained a license to preach in July, 1985 and was ordained in April 1989 at the Antioch Baptist Church in North Hills. PA. She has an earned Bachelor Degree and Masters Degree in Theology and an earned Doctorate Degree in Ministry awarded by Slidell Baptist Seminary.

She was installed as Pastor of St. Paul's Baptist Church on May 18, 2014.
