NAACP in Norristown - 2016

The Coalition of Montgomery County Pennsylvania Branches of the NAACP

On Monday, August 22, 2016 The Coalition of Montgomery County Pennsylvania Branches of the NAACP met on the steps of Montgomery County Courthouse. The Coalition is seeking collaboration with Montgomery County Police departments (Ambler, Cheltenham, Main Line, Norristown, Pottstown and Willow Grove) to address the causes of the divide between police and the communities that they serve. We are committed to working together to improve relationships between our police departments and the communities that they serve. (Taken from the Statement read by John Milligan, President of the Norristown Branch)
