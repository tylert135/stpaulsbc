Thoughts and Reflection

Church (Finance) Meeting
12/5/2011

A congregational meeting was held on Monday, December 5, 2011. The primary focus of the meeting was to provide a financial summary of the past year and a budget proposal for the upcoming year, 2012. This was a very informative meeting in that it has been quite some time since a finance meeting was conducted. That being said, it was not without some concerns. The discussion moved a bit and centered on committees and organizational hierarchy.Other issues discussed was the process of submitting a proposal and vouchers for 2012 events and programs supported by St. Pauls Baptist Church. Budget proposals for the year 2012 are to be submitted by the end of February 2012 for review and approval by church leadership. Forms have been added to the website for ministries to submit. The meeting closed with a prayer.
