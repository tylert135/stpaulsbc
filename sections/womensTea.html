Women's Day Tea - 2016

Women's Tea

On Saturday, September 17, 2016, St. Paul's hosted a Women's Day Tea! Women from various churches attended the tea wearing their best hats and dress clothes. Our key verse came from Psalm 34:1 "I will bless the Lord at all times. His praise will continually be in my mouth". Sister Paula Harris was our guest speaker, and did a phenomenal job sharing what it means to worship, praise, and pray. Sister Lauren and Sister Lindsay Thomas used their angelic voices to praise and worship our Lord during the tea. Tea sandwiches and other goodies were served, as well as hot tea served in real tea cups! Each woman was given a charge to read a scripture each day and to record a reflection until our Women's Day service which will be held on Sunday, October 23, 2016. Lots of door prizes were given to our guests and each woman received a parting gift to thank them for their attendance. Praise the Lord for allowing us to have such a blessed event! More Photos
