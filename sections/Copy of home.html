Praise The Lord Everybody!

Today was our first cancellation of service in 2015. We praise and thank God for His goodness and mercy. The Lord allowed me to drive to church this morning and slide on fourth street just as I made the turn. Then I could not park on the hill and walk into the church; it was so slippery. It was so icy. There were accidents everywhere. But God allowed me to drive back home safely. We can still say that God answers prayer. The St. Paul's church family was praying and God answered our prayers. Our theme this year is "It's Praying Time." So today, please take a little time and pray. On this rainy, icy Sunday, count your blessings. We have so much to be thankful for; God has blessed us in so many ways.

Thank Him for food, shelter, family, friends, health, and so much more. Give Him glorious praise. Don't give "the game" more excitement, laughter, high five waves, etc., than we give to Almighty God!

See you Wednesday night at 6:00 for Prayer Meeting and Bible Study!

Blessings.
Pastor Marjorie Duncan Reed

Thank you for visiting the St. Paul's Baptist Church of Conshohocken online ministry. We are a Bible believing church for all in need. We strive to enrich the spiritual growth of it's members and the community.

As a church family, we are trying to become the kind of church described in the Bible, where there is relevant teaching, heart-felt worship, honest friendships, constant prayer and compassionate care for those in need. We would like to have a contagious faith that will influence and encourage all, one life at a time.

Our church doors are open to people of all backgrounds regardless of where they are on their spiritual journey. So whether you are a spiritual seeker who is just starting to ask questions about God and faith, or a committed Christian who wants to grow in the Lord, you can find a home here at St. Paul's Baptist Church of Conshohocken.

What We Believe


THE LORD JESUS CHRIST
We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God, God manifested in the flesh, true God and true man. We believe in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and in His personal return in power and glory.


EXPRESSIONS OF PRAISE
I will bless the Lord at all times; His praise shall continually be in my mouth. 
My soul will make its boast in the Lord; The humble will hear it and rejoice. 
O magnify the Lord with me, And let us exalt His name together.

Psalm 34: 1-3

THE SCRIPTURES
We believe the Bible to be the inspired Word of God, inerrant in the original writings, the complete revelation of God's will for man and the final authority for Christian faith and conduct.


THE ONE TRUE GOD
We believe in one God, the Creator of all things, eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, who are co-equal and infinitely perfect.
